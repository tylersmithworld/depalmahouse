# Depalma House Catalog Design System

## Philosophy
Everything Depalma makes in one chronological timeline. Art-first, minimal type. Not just a record label page—a creative house portfolio spanning music, products, video production, placements, and studio work.

## Layout
- Single column, full-width images
- Light cream background (#F5F5F0)
- Header: `depalma.house`
- Chronological order (newest first), grouped by year
- Videos cropped to 4:3 aspect ratio to match album art proportions
- No dropdowns or nested navigation—one flat layer

## Right-Side Labels (Type Field)
Labels indicate Depalma's role/relationship to the work:

| Content Type | Label |
|-------------|-------|
| Singles & Albums | `Depalma House Records` |
| Videos filmed at the studio | `Filmed at Depalma` |
| Videos produced by Depalma | `prod by Depalma House` |
| Cable/audio products | `Depalma Cables` |
| Sample packs | `Analog Samples` |
| Studio/house IG posts | `Depalma House` |
| Sponsored projects | `Sponsored by Depalma House` |
| TV/Film placements | Platform name (e.g., `Apple TV+`) |
| DJ mixes/Radio | `Depalma House` |
| Visualizers | `Depalma House Records` |

## Title/Subtitle Structure
- **Title**: Main name (song title, product name, video title)
- **Artist line**: Secondary info OR artist name
  - For music: Artist name + featuring artists on same line, same font
  - For products: Description/specs as subtitle
  - For videos: Artist or series name
- **Featuring artists**: Always on same line as artist, not separate meta line

## Typography
- Inter font family
- Title: 1rem, 500 weight
- Subtitle/Artist: 0.875rem, 400 weight, #666
- Labels: 0.7rem, uppercase, letter-spaced, #999
- Year headers: 2.5rem, 300 weight, #ccc

## Content Categories in Catalog
1. Music releases (Depalma House Records)
2. Music videos filmed at studio
3. Videos produced by Depalma
4. Sample packs (Analog Samples)
5. Handmade cables (Depalma Cables)
6. TV/Film sync placements
7. Instagram posts (studio, car, etc.)
8. DJ mixes / Radio episodes
9. Visualizers
